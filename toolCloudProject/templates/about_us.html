About ToolCloud

ToolCloud is the easiest way to share tools with your neighbors.

Create your account in seconds, all it takes is a few clicks. It's that simple!

Add tools with the navigation bar, or browse your community for other sheds and users.

New tools are added to your personal shed.

Borrow tools from other users in your community, lend them out, or both!

Get started now by clicking register on the navigation bar to your left!
